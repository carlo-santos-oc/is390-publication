The Great Resignation

By the Numbers

A Historic number of people are leaving their jobs. This phenomenon can be attributed to a number of reasons related to work-life balance and seeking new opportunities. This phenomenon has disproportionately affected the tech industry. Tech workers are realizing that they are fully capable of doing their work without having to come to the office. This paradigm shift in office work clashes with management who believe that coming to the office to work is still necessary. The current gulf between job openings and qualified workers is still wide which allows tech workers to have the mobility to seek new opportunities if their current employers do not provide the work-life balance they desire. Does this trend represent a change in the way traditional office work is conducted? Or will things return to the status quo in the future? Below are some statistics on the current situation.
